DPS meets with manufactures and vendors to negotiate product pricing on your behalf.

We have secured exclusive deals with vendors and manufacturers to maintain locked pricing for items on an annual basis, that way you know exactly what you are spending. 

We work with multiple practices through out Ontario and therefore have a large group purchasing power. We have obtained reduced pricing due our relationships and volume of requests. While our vendor partners enjoy the volume of sales, this create a win-win situation for both clinics and vendors. 

We function on a two week ordering cycle, which maintains a consistent product flow.

Staff ordering should take approximately 1 to 2 hours every second week. This would include inventory taking and inputting requests.

At the end of each month, DPS will provide a purchase report for you to evaluate. This will give you the ability to adjust your spending based on budgetary needs. 

Purchasing can be a lengthy process. Staff must search thru flyers and catalogues and make multiple phone calls just to obtain pricing. With DPS we do all the work for you, to free up your staff for patient related duties which in turn creates production for your clinic. All the while we create a cost saving for your purchases.
