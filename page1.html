About Us

We established our Services in 2011. A dental group of five clinics discovered that exclusive vendor purchasing was limiting them in their discounts. It was then a strategy to represent this group and manage all their sundry needs, was created. We sought out multiple vendors to provide for and this established a win-win scenario for both clinic and vendor. This model has sustained us since and the group has tripled in size.

We work with multiple vendors and manufactures to establish the best price to product. We can tailor your product selection that will work directly with your individual needs. All products are HAND selected to purchase by a DPS agent.
